Boo-Bees Crochet Pattern

Written by Nicole Gaddis

Materials:

  • 4-medium-Worsted weight yarn in Black, White, and Yellow
  • 2.75mm Crochet Hook
  • 12mm Saftey Eyes
  • 18 Gauge Wire
  • Darning Needle
  • Hot Glue Gun and Hot Glue

  • Needle Nose pliers (anything that can cut and help shape the wire for the antennas)

  • Optional: Lighter to burn yarn ends and to secure safety eyes
Abbreviations
mcMagic Circle/Ring
scSingle Crochet
dcDouble Crochet
inc Increase
decInvisible Decrease
BLO Back Loop Only
FLO Front Loop Only
Sl st Slip Stitch
chChain
( ) x # Repeat what ever is in the paranthesis the number of times indicated
